# Privacy Policy for Chorda

**Last updated:** 2026-01-30

## Introduction

Chorda (“the App”) is a music composition tool designed to help users create and export chord progressions. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App does — and does not — collect.

---

## Data Collection

Chorda **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

Specifically:

* No personal information is collected
* No usage analytics are collected
* No account or login is required
* No data is sent to external servers

All functionality runs **locally on your device**.

---

## Audio & File Access

Chorda may:

* Generate audio in real time for playback
* Export MIDI or audio files to your device
* Allow you to share exported files using Android’s system share features

All generated files remain under your control and are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them using another app.

---

## Internet Access

If the App requests internet access, it is used only for:

* System-level functionality required by Android
* Future optional features (if any)

No personal or identifiable data is transmitted.

---

## Third-Party Services

Chorda does **not** use:

* Analytics services
* Advertising networks
* Third-party SDKs that collect user data

---

## Children’s Privacy

Chorda does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
